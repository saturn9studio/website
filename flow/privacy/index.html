Privacy Policy for Flow

Your words are your own. We're not interested in them.

Flow doesn't collect, track, analyze, store, peek at, whisper about, or even think about your data.

  • No ads
  • No tracking
  • No analytics
  • No hidden surprises

Everything you write stays on your device, where it belongs.

We built Flow to be a calm, private space for writing—not another data-hungry app. So rest easy. Your words are safe, and your privacy is intact.
